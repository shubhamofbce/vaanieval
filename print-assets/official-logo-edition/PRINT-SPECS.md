# VaaniEval official-logo polo print specifications

## Included variants

- Light-shirt front and back: for white, white-melange, pale grey, and other light garments.
- Dark-shirt front and back: for black, charcoal, navy, and other dark garments.

## Recommended dimensions

- Front chest: 90 mm wide, 60 mm artwork canvas.
- Back: 280 mm wide, 330 mm artwork canvas.

The front print should be positioned on the wearer's left chest. Center the back print approximately 75–90 mm below the collar seam.

## File guidance

- PNG files are standalone, transparent, and exported at 300 DPI.
- SVG files are editable and reference the included official-logo PNG files. Keep the files together when opening the SVGs.
- The dark-shirt variant uses off-white typography and retains the official logo glow.
- The light-shirt variant uses charcoal typography and a cleaned logo edge to avoid a dark halo on white fabric.

The supplied official logo source is preserved in the package for reference. Do not recolor, distort, or redraw it.
